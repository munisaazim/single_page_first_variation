Our story

Welcome to the restaurant, your number one source for all quality food. We are dedicated to giving you the very best of meals, with a focus on custome service and uniqueness.

Founded in 2015 by Munisa, Le Provans Restaurant has come a long way from its beginnings in America. When Munisa first started out, her passion for providing the most tasty food for visitors reason gave her the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration.We now serve large number of people, and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, wing of the restaurant industry.
